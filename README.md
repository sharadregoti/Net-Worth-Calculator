# M-Money

M-Money is a personal finance managment app for android.

M-Money has an inbuit expense tracker, tracks your online spends by reading bank transaction SMS. Connects with your favourite stock broker & crypto account to fetch your asset info. It can also calculate your available bank balance.

Currently available on the Play store and [signal.org](https://signal.org/android/apk/).
